Weaving Game (Unity)

This 3D game was created as a thesis project for a museum environment, allowing users to try a traditional weaving process using a loom. The game guides the player step by step and simulates the work stages with animations.

Technologies: Unity, C#, Blender

Road Trip Survival

A 2D pixel-art challenge game where the player avoids obstacles and collects points. Includes DOS-style difficulty screens and humorous audio.

Technologies: Unity, C#, Aseprite

Visual Novel: Kuruchane

An experimental short story where you care for a silent being that does not move, but reflects your choices and emotions. The player experiences light and darkness, memories and hidden secrets.

Technologies: Ren'Py, GIMP, Audacity
